TRAINING DETAILS
---------------------------------------------

Training Strategy: Two-Phase Transfer Learning
  Phase 1: Base Training (Frozen Backbone)
    - Epochs: 20
    - Learning Rate: 1e-3
    - Trains only classification head
    
  Phase 2: Fine-Tuning (Unfrozen)
    - Epochs: 10
    - Learning Rate: 1e-5
    - Fine-tunes entire model

Data Split:
  - Training: 70% (12,600 images)
  - Validation: 15% (2,700 images)
  - Test: 15% (2,700 images)
  - Stratified split ensures balanced representation

Hardware:
  - GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090
  - CUDA: Enabled
  - Batch Size: 32
